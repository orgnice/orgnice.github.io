## Case Study 1: Marketing Measurement Transformation

**Problem**  
Marketing teams were relying on conflicting ROAS numbers across platforms, leading to poor budget decisions.

**Approach**
- Built robust measurement frameworks
- Introduced incrementality & attribution controls
- Aligned analytics with finance and leadership KPIs

**Impact**
- Clear understanding of true revenue drivers
- Confident budget reallocation
- Reduced waste in high-spend channels

---

## Case Study 2: Scaling Data Science for Growth Teams

**Problem**  
Rapid growth created data chaos — dashboards everywhere, trust nowhere.

**Approach**
- Designed end-to-end analytics architecture
- Simplified metrics to decision-grade KPIs
- Mentored internal analytics teams

**Impact**
- Faster decision cycles
- Leadership trust restored
- Analytics embedded into planning, not reporting

---

## Case Study 3: AI-Driven Business Insights

**Problem**  
Businesses wanted AI solutions but lacked clarity on *where* AI actually adds value.

**Approach**
- Identified high-ROI AI use cases
- Built practical, explainable models
- Integrated insights into existing workflows

**Impact**
- Tangible business outcomes
- Reduced AI experimentation cost
- Sustainable adoption, not demos
